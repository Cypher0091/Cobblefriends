Place 64x64 player skin PNG files in this folder.
Skins will be randomly selected for Battle Tower trainers.

The mod comes with 9 default skins.
Any skins you add here will be added to the pool alongside the defaults.

Supported formats:
- Standard Minecraft player skins (64x64 pixels)
- Both slim and classic models supported
- Any filename ending in .png will work

Note: These skins are loaded from YOUR client.
Other players will see trainers with their own custom skins.
